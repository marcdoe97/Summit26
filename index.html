Frequently Asked Questions

Who is SUMMIT 26 for?
+

SUMMIT 26 is designed for engineers, product leaders, CTOs, researchers, and anyone working on or investing in automation, AI, and autonomous systems technologies.

Where exactly is the event held?
+

Die Veranstaltung findet an der Hochschule Mainz – University of Applied Sciences, Lucy-Hillebrand-Straße 2, 55128 Mainz statt.

Will sessions be recorded?
+

Yes. All keynotes and main stage sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees within 2 weeks of the event. Workshop recordings are available to workshop ticket holders.

Is there a student or startup discount?
+

Yes, we offer a 40% discount for students with valid university ID, and a startup rate for companies under 2 years old with fewer than 10 employees. Use the message field in the registration form to request this.

How do I become a sponsor or speaker?
+

Sponsorship packages and speaking applications are open. Please reach out directly at hello@summit26.com with your proposal or company details.
